## What to collect

Collect 10–20 screenshots, then narrow them down to 5.

Choose screenshots that show:

- sidebar layout
- KPI cards
- dense but readable tables
- status badges
- upload workflow
- warning/error panels
- report/export cards
- clean chart layouts

## Selection checklist

For each screenshot, ask:

- Does it look professional and trustworthy?
- Would an operations manager understand it quickly?
- Are tables readable?
- Are status badges clear?
- Does it avoid fake complexity?
- Can I adapt it to order validation, allocation, payment aging, and reports?
- Can I explain why I chose this design in an interview?
